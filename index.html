The Ossetian Syndicate

With a bloodied history in three eras of warfare, this powerful Syndicate became a military powerhouse. Through genetic splicing, the Ossetian Syndicate often rely on enhanced beings, hybrids, to safeguard it's existence.

  • Learn More

Tania Sarazen Storchovnyiva

The daughter of Dmitriy Storchovnyiv and heir apparent to the Ossetian Syndicate, Tania is the stellar femme fatale of the criminal underworld, having learned three martial arts forms and is proficient in firearms.

  • Learn More

The Tudori Syndicate

What they lack in numbers, they make up for in money. They may be the least populous of all factions, but make no mistake. The Tudoris are masters of grand strategy, open-ended warfare, and have hid their true motives until now.

  • Learn More

Michelle Calitri

Sister to Aberfeldy Calitri, this crash survivor faces an existential crisis. Hounded by her amnesia of the crash, Michelle is surrounded by enemies as she seeks to rebuild the Tudoris into a terrifying Syndicate unlike before.
